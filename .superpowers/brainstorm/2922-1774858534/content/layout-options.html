B. Wizard Flow (Step 1 → 2 → 3 → Results)

Guided step-by-step: Model → Hardware → Training → Results. Focused, one thing at a time. Good for first-time users. But slower for experts who want to tweak one param.

C. Top Bar Config + Full Results Page

Config is a compact summary bar at top (click to expand/edit). Results dominate the page. Fastest for iterating — change one param, see results instantly. Like a trading terminal.

Recommendation: A (Side-by-Side) is the most natural for a parameter-exploration tool where you constantly tweak and compare. C is great if screen real estate for charts matters most.
